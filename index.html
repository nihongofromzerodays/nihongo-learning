Welcome to Day 1

Congratulations on starting your Japanese learning journey. Today is special because it's the day you stop thinking "I can't" and start proving "I can."

We're going to learn five characters. Just five. That's all.

What is Hiragana?

Hiragana is one of the three writing systems used in Japanese. Think of Hiragana as the "basic alphabet" of Japanese.

Every Japanese person learns Hiragana first, usually around age 5 or 6. Hiragana has 46 basic characters. But today, we're only learning 5.
